Featured Publication

What are we?

What Are We? explores the uncertain space between friendship and love — the emotions that exist when two people care deeply for each other but struggle to define what they truly are. The story focuses on quiet moments, emotional hesitation, and the comfort found within unspoken connections.

The design was created to feel personal, youthful, and emotionally intimate. Bright yellow tones were used to represent warmth, nostalgia, and the comforting energy shared between the characters, while the handwritten typography adds a raw, diary-like quality to the publication. The layouts remain minimal and spacious to keep the focus on expressions, pauses, and emotional tension, allowing the visuals to communicate feelings that words often cannot express.

Year 2025 Design and Illustration Bhumi Chopra Medium Print and Digital Publication Category Book Design
