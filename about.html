Bhumi Chopra

Designer & Filmmaker, New Delhi

I am a visual storyteller and designer who expresses emotions through design, animation, and cinematic storytelling. My work blends scriptwriting, branding, and visual communication to create narratives that feel personal, immersive, and emotionally driven.

From developing brand identities to creating visual experiences across different mediums, I see design as more than aesthetics — it is the language I use to translate thoughts, silence, and emotions into meaningful narratives. Curiosity, adaptability, and storytelling continue to shape the way I approach every creative project.

My approach is rooted in observation, experimentation, and adaptability, which helps me move fluidly between branding, digital design, animation, film, and visual communication. For me, every project is an opportunity to create work that not only looks visually compelling but also leaves behind a feeling, atmosphere, or story that stays with people.
