Animated Short Film

DEEWAR

Voh kahani jisne kabhi nahi sone diya

A young girl grows up believing the walls of her home are ordinary, until they begin to reveal the stories hidden behind closed doors. Through shadows, silence, and fading memories, she discovers that while some families fall apart, a mother's love can quietly build a home all over again.

Deewar explores how children understand domestic conflict through the silence, shadows, and emotions hidden behind closed doors. Using 2D animation and symbolic visual storytelling, the wall becomes a silent witness to untold memories. The film is ultimately a journey from fear and loss to healing, hope, and unconditional love.
